The paper that inspired me to plot the volatility cone is
http://www.m-x.ca/f_publications_en/cone_vol_en.pdf

Rchart package is used for plotting the data, and the option chain and stock data are obtained 
using quantmod and flipsideR packages. Highcharts, which is built on javascript is used for the plotting.

Implied Volatility is directly proportional to the Option premium and this chart can let the traders
and analyst visually observe the IV of options with respect to the rolling volatility at different time
to maturity
